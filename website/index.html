üìã Installation & Setup Guide

Step 1: Install the App

  1. Download the DMG file above
  2. Double-click MacCleanup-1.0.dmg to open it
  3. Drag MacCleanup.app to your Applications folder
  4. Double-click MacCleanup.app to launch it

Step 2: First-Time Configuration

  1. Right-click MacCleanup.app ‚Üí Show Package Contents
  2. Navigate to: Contents/Resources/
  3. Open Terminal in that folder: cd /Applications/MacCleanup.app/Contents/Resources
    python3 setup_wizard.py
  4. The wizard will:
    • Detect your Dropbox folder (if you have one)
    • Detect your external drives (if any)
    • Ask you to configure paths
    • Save your configuration

‚öôÔ∏è Configuration Options

You can use:

  • Dropbox (detected automatically)
  • iCloud Drive (select in wizard)
  • No cloud storage (works fine locally)
  • External drives (detected automatically, optional)

The app organizes YOUR files on YOUR Mac. It doesn't access anyone else's files or any server.

Step 3: Launch & Use

  1. Double-click MacCleanup.app to launch
  2. Your browser opens automatically at http://localhost:5050
  3. Click "Preview Cleanup" to see suggested actions
  4. Select/deselect actions using checkboxes
  5. Click "Run Cleanup" to execute selected actions

üîÑ Using MacCleanup in the Future

Every time you want to clean up your files:

  1. Double-click MacCleanup.app in your Applications folder
  2. Your browser opens at http://localhost:5050
  3. Click "Preview Cleanup" to see what needs organizing
  4. Review the suggestions
  5. Select the actions you want (using checkboxes)
  6. Click "Run Cleanup" to execute

That's it! The app runs locally on your Mac - no internet connection needed, no server, just your files.
